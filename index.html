Introduction

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a track-focused sports car that delivers unparalleled performance and driving experience. With a lightweight design and powerful engine, it epitomizes what a true sports car should be.

Specifications

  • Engine: Six-cylinder naturally aspirated boxer engine with gasoline particulate filter
  • Power: 518 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 3.0 seconds
  • Top Speed: 197 mph
  • Weight: 1,450 kg (3,197 lbs)

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS has evolved through several generations since its introduction in 2003, with each model enhancing track performance and aerodynamics. The key generations include the 996, 997, 991, and the current 992 GT3 RS, each featuring more power, lighter materials, and advanced technology.
